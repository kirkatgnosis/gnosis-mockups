THE FOUR TRAPS
  • Interpretation: an assumption, not evidence
  • Exact words, wrong context: right words, wrong place
  • Reverse relationship: facts right, direction flipped
  • Part true, part not: one unsupported clause
01 ·

Read what is asked

The question wants what Fran believes about her bond with Linda Rose, not what any single line literally says.

02 ·

Strike the traps

Three choices lift real words from the passage but fail it. Name how each one breaks before you cross it out.

03 ·

Confirm with the line

Only A survives. The homing pigeon and the Dallas postmark show a bond stretched across distance, so the ∴ marks the evidence and the underline draws.
